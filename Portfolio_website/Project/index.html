If you don't have the willpower to put your smartphone down once

and for all, this lamp will give you that extra push. WeBloom is

an interactive piece of lighting where the user's phone acts as a

kind of light switch. We wanted to focus on the work productivity

related to mobile phone interruptions and various distractions that

possibly occur. WeBloom shows how the mobile phone can be used not only

as a lighting element, but as a way to bring a study group closer together.
